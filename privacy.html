Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Privacy by Design: This public website presents research and capabilities only. It does not provide billing, purchases, or product access.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Foltz.ai ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the Foltz.ai public website and research materials ("the Site"). We are committed to protecting privacy and designing local-first legal AI research with explicit data boundaries.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The public Site does not collect, access, process, or store any of the following:

  • Personal Data: No names, addresses, Social Security numbers, immigration data, financial information, or any other personally identifiable information
  • Documents: No uploaded files, scanned images, PDFs, or OCR output
  • Usage Analytics: No telemetry, usage statistics, crash reports, or behavioral tracking
  • Device Information: No hardware identifiers, IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or geolocation data
  • Cookies & Tracking: No cookies, web beacons, pixels, or third-party trackers

3. How Local Research Builds Work

Foltz.ai research prioritizes local processing:

  • OCR Processing: Document scanning and text extraction runs locally using embedded OCR engines
  • AI Analysis: All AI models run locally on your CPU/GPU — no API calls to external AI services
  • Form Generation: PDF form filling and output generation happens entirely on your device
  • Data Storage: Any case data, matter records, or generated documents are stored only in your local filesystem under your control

Current local research builds are designed for no-network verifier paths. Exact network behavior depends on the build and enabled integrations, which are gated outside the public website.

Automated Decision-Making Technology (ADMT)

Foltz.ai utilizes small language models (SLMs) to assist with form population and auditing. Your data is never used to train or improve these models. The models run deterministically on your local hardware. We do not aggregate your client inputs, nor do we perform any systematic observation, profiling, or background dataset iteration. Our AI architecture ensures complete compliance with California regulations regarding ADMT and AI data usage.

Human Review Required: While your data stays private and local, all AI-generated output MUST be proofread and verified by a qualified human before use. Foltz.ai does not condone the use of AI-generated content without thorough human review. Foltz.ai materials can assist preparation and research — never replace professional judgment.

4. Local Data Storage

Local research builds may create local files on your device, including:

  • Application configuration and preference files
  • Locally cached AI model files
  • Generated PDF documents and audit logs
  • Session recovery data for interrupted workflows

All such files remain on your device and under your exclusive control. You may delete these files at any time. Foltz.ai has no ability to access, read, or recover data from your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Foltz.ai's local research workflows do not require third-party services, cloud providers, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Optional integrations, such as e-filing or signature providers, must be enabled explicitly and are outside the local-first demo path.

6. Children's Privacy

The Site and any research builds are not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Since the public Site does not collect personal information for legal matter workflows, this concern is inherently addressed by our architecture.

7. Data Security

Because Foltz.ai operates entirely offline and does not collect or transmit data, the security of your information depends on the security of your local device and operating system. We recommend that you:

  • Keep your operating system and security software up to date
  • Use full-disk encryption on devices containing sensitive legal documents
  • Follow your organization's data security policies and applicable ethical obligations
  • Restrict physical access to devices used for local research builds

8. Your Rights & Data Subject Access Requests (DSAR)

Under the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and similar legislation, you have fundamental rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, delete, and restrict processing.

DSAR Compliance: Foltz.ai operates on a principle of absolute data minimization. Because the public Site does not collect, store, or process any telemetry, user data, or personal information on our servers, there is no personal information for us to disclose, delete, correct, or port. You retain full ownership, access, and deletion control of all data by simply managing the files on your local device. If you wish to submit a formal DSAR inquiry, please contact our compliance team at privacy@foltz.ai.

9. Website Logs

If you visit our website, standard web server logs may record your IP address as part of the HTTP request. This is a standard function of web server infrastructure and is not unique to Foltz.ai. We do not correlate these logs with any personal identity, legal matter, or product usage information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Any changes will be posted on our website with a revised "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California, United States, without regard to conflict of law principles.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@foltz.ai.
